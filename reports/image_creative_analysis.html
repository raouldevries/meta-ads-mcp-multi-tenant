🎯 For Echt Location

Reshoot creative to show gym interior with Milon circuit. Keep the welcoming staff member but photograph inside the facility with equipment visible.

📸 Creative Best Practices

Always include: Gym interior, equipment, female subjects (target demo), motivational elements. Avoid: Outdoor-only shots, seasonal themes, unclear value proposition.
